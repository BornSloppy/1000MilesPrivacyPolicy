Privacy Policy for 1000 Miles
1000 Miles is designed to help walkers track their miles in a simple, private, and offline way. We believe your data belongs to you, so we keep things straightforward.
Information We Collect
•	Name (optional): You can enter your first name or full name to personalise certificates and parts of the app.
•	Walking data: You can manually enter miles walked, and the app stores your totals and history.
How Your Data Is Stored
•	All data — including your name and walking history — is stored only on your device.
•	Nothing is sent to us, to servers, or to any third parties.
What We Don’t Collect
•	No email address, phone number, or personal profile information.
•	No GPS or location data.
•	No automatic activity tracking.
•	No analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies.
Internet Use
•	The app works fully offline.
•	It does not connect to the internet or transmit any data.
Data Sharing
•	We do not share your data with anyone.
•	We do not use third party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.
Data Control
•	You can delete your walking data at any time from within the app.
•	Deleting the app from your device also removes all stored data.
Children’s Privacy
•	The app does not collect personal information beyond an optional name and is safe for all ages.
Changes to This Policy
If we ever update this policy, we will update the date below.
